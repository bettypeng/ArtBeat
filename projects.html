WORKSHOP // APRIL 27

EARTH WEEK RECYCLED GARDEN

In collaboration with Brown's Earth Week Committee, Artbeat held a workshop where students created a 'recycled garden' out of old aluminum cans. The garden was installed on the main green the next day.

EVENT // APRIL 27

ART & MUSIC NIGHT @ FLATBREAD

As a pre-Spring Weekend event / fundraiser, Artbeat hosted an art and music benefit night at the Flatbread Company just off of Thayer Street. Students groups such as Folkmade, Radical Artists, and MADE by RISD (a student boutique at RISD) made an appearance and sold their artwork to those interested. Multiple talented musicians and singers performed some low key acoustic tunes while students browsed artwork and enjoyed flatbread pizza. Music groups / artists present included:

FUNDRAISER // APRIL 30

SPINART WITH KAPPA DELTA

Artbeat helped out at Kappa Delta's Dunk 'n Dodge fundraiser event. We designed a homemade spinart machine using just wood, nails, cardboard, tarp, and a powered hand drill. Check out some of the absolutely ~awesome~ spin art designs that people made!
